PlayHitsGame

To register, you must fill in the Username field with a valid email address. You will receive an email with a link you need to use to validate the registration, as a security measure to prevent indiscriminate sign-ups.

Fill in the password and confirmation fields, trying to use uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters to make it as secure as possible.

The Group field can be filled with any characters. It is used to create games with other players; all players who enter the same group name can play a group game together. You can leave it blank at registration and fill it in later whenever you wish.

Finally, you can click the send button, and you will receive an email at the address provided in the Username field. Check your spam or junk mail folder, where you will need to click the link to confirm the registration.

Once registration is confirmed, you can click the Access link or go back to the Home page to return to the login and use the email entered as your username along with the password to access the application.
