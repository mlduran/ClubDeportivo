PlayHitsGame

You are about to discover the greatest music jukebox of all time. This game will test your knowledge of the music scene across different styles and eras. You will need to guess, based on listening to a song, its title, artist, and release year. All you’ll need is a PC or mobile device capable of playing music, or for higher quality, you can use a mobile phone connected to a Bluetooth speaker.

You can create solo games to play by yourself, collaborate with friends, or create group games to compete against them. In each game, you can choose a range of years and a theme for the song selection.

This game uses music provided by Spotify. If you have an interesting playlist that we could add to our list of themes or any other suggestions or comments, you can send them to us at our email: info@playhitsgame.es

If you show great knowledge of the music scene, you could enter the leaderboard for each theme, which you can check at any time.

Don’t hesitate!!! Register, this game is completely free. Start playing and test your knowledge of the most iconic songs of all time. If you prefer, you can also access as a guest, but in this case, most of the game’s features won’t be available. In any case, we hope you enjoy the experience.
