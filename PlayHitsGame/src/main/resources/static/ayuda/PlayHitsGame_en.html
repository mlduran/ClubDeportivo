PlayHitsGame

Ready to turn any gathering into an unforgettable experience? With our game, you have the largest jukebox of all time at your fingertips. Test your knowledge and show how much you know about music while having fun with your friends or playing solo.

🎶 What’s the game about?
Listen to song snippets and guess the title, artist, and release year. You can choose from different genres and eras to customize your experience. All you need is a PC or mobile device, and for an even better experience, connect your phone to a Bluetooth speaker for everyone to enjoy.

👥 Game Modes for Everyone

  • Solo or Collaborative: Create personal games to play on your own or with friends.

  • Competitive: Create group games and compete against others to prove who knows the most.

🎧 Customize Your Experience
Choose a range of years or a specific theme for the songs. Additionally, thanks to Spotify's music library, you can send us your favorite playlists to include them in future challenges! Write to us at info@playhitsgame.es with your suggestions.

Show off your knowledge and climb to the top of the leaderboard for each theme.
