Personal Games

In Personal games, the goal is to get the highest number of points possible. To do this, the first challenge is to pass as many rounds as possible (if possible, finishing the game when no more songs are selected for it) and to score the maximum number of points in each round.

At the beginning of each round, you must press the play button on the player (you can also use the spacebar on PC or double-tap on touch screens). Once the song is identified, select the corresponding answers for the Title, Artist, and Release Year. Both the title and the artist will appear with only half of their letters visible, with a wildcard character for the remaining letters.

Only one answer can be selected for each group, and once selected, it cannot be modified. After providing answers to the 3 questions, the system will calculate the points obtained, and if possible, it will proceed to the next round.

Points are awarded based on correct answers:

15 points for getting the Title correct

15 points for getting the Artist correct

15 points for getting the Release Year correct

These are the points if the selected difficulty for the game is Normal. If Easy was chosen, these points are halved, and if Difficult was chosen, they are doubled. Keep in mind that if the 'Unobfuscated' option was selected, the points based on the level will be divided by 2. For Training difficulty, the points are the same as in Normal, but they do not count toward updating records for the Topic.

These points are reduced by the time taken to answer, considering that there is a 15-second countdown from when the round loads, which does not reduce points and should be used to start the playback and listen to part of the song. For example, a Normal round where the Artist and Year were correct, and 13 seconds were used (after the 15-second countdown), will score 17 points.

For each round played, information will be added with the answers, and if you click on the cover, you can play the song again.

To advance to the next round, the chosen difficulty will be taken into account:

Training: This level allows playing without ending the game until the button is pressed to do so. It is only active in this type of game.

Normal: In this level, you must get at least 2 out of the 3 answers correct to move on to the next round and continue the game.

Easy: At this level, you need to get at least 1 out of the 3 answers correct.

Difficult: Here you can demonstrate your full potential, as you must not get any of the answers wrong.
