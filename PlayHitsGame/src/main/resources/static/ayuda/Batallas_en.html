Battles

What are musical battles?

Musical battles are competitions where you can interact with other registered players to prove who is the best. Each day, a new battle with a specific theme will open, and you will have 24 hours to register. On the main panel, once you access, you will find information about the available battle and a link to join it. Each battle will allow a maximum of 32 players.

Once registration is closed, all players will have 24 hours to play their match. At the end of this period, half of the players with the most points will advance to the next phase. In this new phase, you will have another 24 hours to play another match, and so on, until only one winner remains. This winner will receive a new star for their record.

Like group matches, battles will consist of several rounds. The number of rounds will increase as players advance through the different phases of the competition.

How to win a battle?

In battles, there can only be one winner. Your goal is to be that player. To achieve this, you must score as many points as possible in each round of the match and progress phase by phase until you reach the final and defeat your last opponent.

Round dynamics:

At the start of each round, press the play button on the player to begin (you can also use the space bar on a PC or double-tap on touch screens if you’ve enabled this option in “Edit User Data”).

Once you identify the song, select the correct answers to the following questions:

Title

Performer

Release year

Important details:

Both the title and the performer will appear with only half of their letters visible; the rest will be covered by wildcard characters.

You can only select one answer per question, and once selected, it cannot be changed.

When you complete your answers, the system will calculate the points earned and indicate whether you advance to the next round.

Scoring system:

15 points for correctly identifying the title.

15 points for correctly identifying the performer.

15 points for correctly identifying the release year.

This score will be reduced by the time taken to respond, considering that you have a 15-second countdown at the start of each round to play the song and listen to part of it.

Scoring example: If you correctly identify the performer and the release year but take an additional 13 seconds after the initial 15 seconds, you will earn a score of 17 points.

Additional information:

For each round played, you can review the details of your answers. Additionally, if you click on the cover art, you can replay the song.

For each battle phase, you will receive an email with the results. If you do not wish to receive these emails, you can disable them in the “Edit User Data” option.

Stars

Stars are a symbol of prestige in PlayHitsGame. You can earn them in two ways:

Setting a record in any of the proposed themes in Personal Matches.

Winning a battle.

There are only 100 stars available. Each time a player earns a new star, it will be taken from the player with the oldest star.
